Grill Chicken
Whole chicken (around 1.5-2 kg) or chicken pieces.
3 tbsp olive oil or melted butter.
1 tbsp garlic (minced).
2 tsp paprika or red chili powder.
1 tsp turmeric powder.
1 tsp black pepper powder.
1 tbsp dried or fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme, or oregano).
1 tbsp lemon juice.
Salt to taste.
Vegetables for roasting (optional: carrots, potatoes, onions).
Lemon wedges and fresh parsley (optional garnish).
